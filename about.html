About Us

We’re Everyday AI for Seniors—Janie Mah, Sharon Tom, and Tim Fisher—three educators and professionals passionate about helping older adults embrace technology with ease and FUN! Together, we bring decades of experience spanning digital learning, community outreach, senior empowerment and tech support for beginners.

Our Mission

Our mission is simple: to empower seniors to get up to speed with this exciting new technology – Artificial Intelligence or AI for short. We enable seniors to bring AI into their daily lives safely, confidently, and creatively—whether that’s staying on top of their health, fraud, communicating with family, managing tasks, or just having fun.
